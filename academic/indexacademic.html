Final Exam

Dear students, Academic affairs and Admissions divisions team congratulate all of you with coming Day of Remembrance and Honors (9 May). Our division would like to inform you that Final Examinations for Spring 2018 Semester will be held at the end of semester, namely from May 14 to 18, 2018. Please check the attached Final Examinations schedule and Room schedule where you are assigned.Please take into consideration that you are responsible for learning the times and locations of exams in their courses and for arriving at your exams on time. Students who miss an exam will receive a permanent ABS (unexcused absence), which is equivalent in all respects to a failing grade. Students must have their student’s ID card before the exam begins. If you do not have ID, you will be allowed to attend the exam with passport only Students who are late more than 10 minutes may not be allowed to take the examination; therefore, you are advised to allocate sufficient time for commuting and finding the designated examination rooms. more

Exam Rules

Dear students, Academic affairs and admissions division once again reminding you about the rules during exams As you know, exam duration is one and half hours in length. During final examinations week, exams start at 9:30 a.m. and the end at 5:00 p.m. You should adhere to the rules which are given in the attached file during the administration of scheduled final examinations For violation of the examination rules or dishonesty in an examination, a student is reported directly to the Academic Affairs division to take action by Committee of Award and Penalty. Therefore there is no need for students to cheat. Please write down your own answer on test paper. Students who fail to obey the instructions of an examination proctor are liable to disciplinary action.
